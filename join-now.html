How Your Donations Help Protect the Amazon

Every donation to the United Nature Foundation directly supports the protection, restoration, and long-term survival of the Amazon rainforest ecosystem. Your support helps us safeguard one of the most biodiverse places on Earth — protecting wildlife, supporting local guardians, and restoring damaged rainforest for future generations.

Plant and Restore Native Rainforest Trees

Donations help us reforest degraded areas of the Amazon by planting native tree species that restore habitats, improve biodiversity, and strengthen the rainforest's natural ability to absorb carbon.

team planting saplings

Support Frontline Forest Patrols

Your contribution helps fund local teams who monitor and patrol protected rainforest areas to prevent illegal logging, poaching, mining, and land destruction. These patrols are essential for protecting vulnerable wildlife and preserving untouched forest.

patrol vehicle on forest track

Protect Endangered Wildlife Habitats

The Amazon is home to jaguars, macaws, river dolphins, monkeys, sloths, and thousands of rare species. Donations help preserve the habitats these animals rely on to survive. Healthy rainforests protect vital waterways that sustain wildlife, local communities, and the wider global climate system.

jaguar resting in undergrowth

Support Local Communities

We work alongside local people who play a vital role in protecting the rainforest. Donations help create sustainable opportunities that encourage conservation over deforestation. Contributions also help fund educational resources and conservation awareness projects that inspire children and families to value and protect nature.
