Our Mission

United Nature Foundation exists to remove the barriers between people and possibility.

We connect isolated communities to clean energy, safe passage, and a protected natural world — so that every child can reach their classroom, and every generation can inherit healthy land. At United Nature Foundation, we believe that where you live should never determine what you can achieve. We work directly alongside communities to put practical, sustainable solutions in place. We install solar energy systems that bring reliable power to homes and schools for the first time. We provide e-bikes that give children and families a safe, clean way to travel through difficult terrain — whatever the weather. And we work to protect and restore the land itself, standing against deforestation and working with communities to be stewards of the forests, rivers, and ecosystems that surround them.

What We Do

Three projects. One mission.

Every project tackles a different barrier standing between isolated communities and the life they deserve. Together, they form the foundation of lasting change.

Infrastructure

Pathways Project

When floods rise and trees fall, the routes children rely on to reach school disappear. The Pathways Project works to clear, rebuild, and protect safe routes through difficult terrain — so that no child misses school because of the weather.

Transport

Pedal Project

E-bikes give children and families a clean, reliable way to travel through remote landscapes — whatever the conditions. The Pedal Project puts sustainable transport directly into the hands of communities who need it most.

Energy

Power Project

Reliable electricity changes everything — from studying after dark to powering medical equipment. The Power Project installs solar energy systems in off-grid communities, bringing light, warmth, and independence to the people who need it.

Live Rainfall Impact

When the rains come,
every path becomes a barrier.

When the rains come to the Amazon, the landscape transforms overnight. Rivers swell, paths flood, and the journey to school becomes a gauntlet of deep mud, fallen trees, and fast-moving water.

Building safe pathways and bridges for the children of Presidente Figueiredo is one of UNF's most vital humanitarian projects — but we can't do it alone. Your support puts the next safe step under a child's feet.

UNF land purchases does more than support conservation — it directly protects real, living rainforest in Presidente Figueiredo, one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth.

This is a place where jaguars roam, river dolphins glide through winding waterways, and thousands of rare and endangered species thrive in a fragile ecosystem. When you join UNF, you secure a portion of land, permanently safeguarded and transformed into a protected nature reserve in your name.
