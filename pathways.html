Connecting Communities. Creating Opportunity. Protecting the Amazon.

In remote communities throughout Presidente Figueiredo, Brazil, families face challenges that many of us never experience. Seasonal flooding can turn pathways into rivers, isolating communities from schools, healthcare, food supplies, and essential services.

United Nature Foundation exists to help create safe, sustainable connections between people, opportunity, and nature — building a future where communities can thrive while protecting one of the world's most important ecosystems.

Why we started

For many families living within the Amazon region, access is not guaranteed. Heavy rainfall and changing weather conditions can make routes impassable, preventing children from attending school and cutting communities off from vital resources. What should be a simple journey becomes a barrier to education, opportunity, and wellbeing.

We believe no child should miss an education because they cannot safely reach school, and no family should be isolated from essential services.

The Heart of Our Headquarters

Alphonso was the heart of this area of the rainforest. People were drawn to him, protected by him, and inspired by him. That is exactly what our headquarters will become.
