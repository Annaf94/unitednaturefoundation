Welcome to Canopy Crew Kids — the exciting nature club for young rainforest explorers who love animals, adventure, and protecting our planet!

Canopy Crew is an exciting children's membership designed for young nature lovers aged 4–15 who want to help protect the Amazon Rainforest and its incredible wildlife.

For just £8.99 a month, members become part of a fun and inspiring community dedicated to learning about nature, conservation, and endangered animals.

Children will receive monthly rainforest updates, fun educational content, and exciting activities that help them connect with the natural world. Every quarter, members will also receive an exclusive Canopy Crew pack filled with special goodies, collectables, and surprises to continue their rainforest adventure at home.

From wildlife fact files and creative activities to certificates and exclusive member items, Canopy Crew helps children learn, explore, and make a real difference for the planet — all while having fun along the way.
