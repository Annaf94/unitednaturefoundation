Volunteer with
United Nature Foundation

Volunteering with the Amazon School Pathway Project is a rare opportunity to combine hands-on community work with one of the world's most extraordinary natural environments.

You will be working directly alongside local families and communities in the Amazon rainforest, contributing to infrastructure that will change the daily lives of children for generations to come. This is not a passive experience — your skills, energy, and commitment will have a visible, lasting impact.
